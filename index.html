What We Offer

Comprehensive Care
Under One Roof

From routine check-ups to specialised services — our practice is equipped to care for your whole family.

General Practitioners

Five experienced doctors providing comprehensive family GP services for patients of all ages — from newborns to seniors.

Procedure Room

Minor procedures under local anaesthesia and management of minor emergencies in our dedicated on-site procedure room.

Centre for Diabetes

Dedicated diabetic care and comprehensive management for patients of all ages, led by our experienced specialist team.

Skin Specialist

Dr. Ian Duursema available on-site for all dermatology needs. Contact directly: 011 954 4824/5

Lancet Depot

Convenient on-site laboratory and pathology services through the trusted Lancet network — no need to travel separately.

Mental Health & Psychiatry

Compassionate support for mental health, ADHD, and psychiatric care — provided by our dedicated specialist Dr. Potgieter.
